Welcome

Hi, my name is Landon Reaves and I am the Owner/Operator of LHR Excavation & Grading, LLC. I am based out of Newton, NC and serve Catawba County and the surrounding counties as well. I primarily use a Bobcat E50 mini-excavator and specialize in land/lot clearing, small grading projects, tree removal, shrub/bush removal, pond landscaping, gravel or dirt road repair, culvert removal or installation, small building demolition and more! I am a fully insured service provider and always have safety first in mind. Please reach out to me through phone call, text or Email. Thank you!
